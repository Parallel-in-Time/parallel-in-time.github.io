Jülich Supercomputing Centre

The research group at the Jülich Supercomputing Centre is lead by Dr. Robert Speck mentoring PhD students as well as a varying number of master and guest students.

They concentrate on multi-level methods such as MLSDC, PFASST and multigrid methods as well as resilience and HPC implementations.

Currently, they develop and maintain two implementations of these algortihms. One is PFASST++ and the other pySDC.
