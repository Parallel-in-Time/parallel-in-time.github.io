Institute of Computational Science

Group High Performance Methods for Numerical Simulation in Science, Medicine and Engineering.

Members of the group at ICS started working on parallel-in-time methods in the course of the Swiss High Performance and High Productivity Computing initiative HP2C. Research on time-parallel methods is now also part of the ExaSolvers projects.
