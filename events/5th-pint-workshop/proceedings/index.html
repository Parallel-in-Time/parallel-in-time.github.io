Proceedings

Workshop proceedings will be published as a special issue of the Springer journal Computing and Visualization in Science. Papers will undergo the standard review process. LaTeX sources for the journal can be downloaded here.

Submission is now open: Please use the new online submission system available here to submit your paper. Papers should be regular length journal papers (around 10 to 15 pages). Please check out the instructions for authors.

Important: When submitting, please select Proceedings PINT Workshop under Category.

The deadline for the submission is 8 May 2017 (extended deadline from 30 April).

Guest editors for this issue will be

  • Rolf Krause
  • Daniel Ruprecht
  • Jacob Schroder
