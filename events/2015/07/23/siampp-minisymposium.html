Minisymposium at SIAM PP 2016

Plans for a proposal for a PINT mini symposium at SIAM PP 2016 in Paris.

With mini symposia related to parallel-in-time integration at

  • SIAM CS&E 2013 in Boston,
  • SIAM PP 2014 in Savannah,
  • SIAM CS&E 2015 in Salt Lake City

and an invited talk by Rob Falgout also at SIAM CS&E 2015, the topic has been well represented at SIAM conferences in the last years.

To continue these efforts, a proposal for a mini symposium on parallel-in-time integration methods for the upcoming SIAM PP 2016 (April 12-15, 2016) in Paris is in the planning stage. If you would like to contribute to the mini symposium with a presentation, please contact one of the organisers:

  • Tobias Neckel, TU Munich
  • Daniel Ruprecht, USI Lugano
  • Martin Schreiber, University of Exeter

The deadline for proposals is in September, so the list of speakers will have to be finalised towards the end of August.
