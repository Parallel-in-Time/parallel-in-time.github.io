_PinT 2026: the 14th Workshop on Parallel-in-time Integration_ will be hosted at [Johann Radon Institute for Computational and Applied Mathematics (RICAM)](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/ricam/home) from **6. to 10. July 2026**.
It is jointly organized by [Andreas Schafelner (JKU Linz)](https://numa.jku.at/team/schafelner) and [Herbert Egger (JKU Linz & RICAM)](https://numa.jku.at/team/egger).

**Registration and important dates**

Registration is mandatory, but free of charge. Please **submit your abstracts by May 24, 2026** via the [registration form](https://www.ricam.oeaw.ac.at/events/workshops/pint2026/registration). 
Notification of acceptance until June 7, 2026. 

➡️ _More details about program, accomodations how to come on the website : [https://www.ricam.oeaw.ac.at/events/workshops/pint2026](https://www.ricam.oeaw.ac.at/events/workshops/pint2026)_
