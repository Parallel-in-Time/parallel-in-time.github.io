ParaDIAG

ParaDIAG is a class of Parallel-in-Time (PinT) algorithms based on the diagonalization technique [1]. ParaDIAG can handle both the dissipative problems (e.g., the advection-diffusion equations) and the purely wave equations (e.g., the acoustic equation).
